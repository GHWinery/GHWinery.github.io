Opening the Square POS and Cash Register

  1. Start drawer Register 1 with $300.
  2. Start drawer Register 2 with $200.
    Cash amounts depend on season, sometimes registers have more.
  3. If necessary, make change in drawers.
    Bill Count
    $20 1
    $10 9
    $5 15
    $1 75
    25c, 10c, 5c, 1c 1 roll of each
  4. Document the number of bills and coins on the Opening POS Sheet below the cash drawer.
    Total should be $300.
  5. Repeat counting and documenting steps for Register 2.
