Wine and Store Supply Orders

Follow these procedures when placing orders for wine or store supplies.

Store locations can place an order once a week for any needed wines or supplies. Each Store Lead or Manager can complete the Winery Supply Order Form on Google Sheets to place an order. The form will be sent to Production or Ordering for fulfillment.
