# Facility Overview

## Winery Layout

Understanding our facility layout is essential for efficient operations and safety. This section provides an overview of the main areas and their functions.

## Main Areas

### Production Area

**Location:** Main building, ground floor

The production area houses our primary winemaking equipment:

- Crush pad and receiving area
- Destemmer and crusher
- Press systems
- Fermentation tank farm
- Pump stations

**Access:** Authorized personnel only during active operations

### Cellar

**Location:** Main building, lower level

Temperature-controlled space for aging and storage:

- Barrel room (55-60Â°F)
- Tank storage
- Bottled wine storage
- Racking and blending station

**Climate Control:** Monitored 24/7

### Laboratory

**Location:** Main building, second floor

Quality control and testing facility:

- Chemical analysis equipment
- Sensory evaluation room
- Sample storage
- Office space for records

**Access:** Lab-trained personnel only

### Bottling Line

**Location:** Separate building, east wing

Bottling and packaging operations:

- Bottle washing station
- Filling line
- Corking/capping equipment
- Labeling station
- Case packing area

### Warehouse

**Location:** Separate building, west side

Storage and shipping:

- Finished goods storage
- Shipping and receiving dock
- Dry goods storage
- Equipment storage

## Utilities and Support Systems

### Water Systems
- Municipal water supply
- Wastewater treatment
- Hot water system for sanitation

### Electrical
- Main panel location: North wall of production area
- Emergency shutoffs clearly marked
- Backup generator for critical systems

### Compressed Air
- Compressor room: East side of production
- Air lines throughout facility
- Filters and dryers for product contact

### Refrigeration
- Glycol chiller system
- Cooling jacket connections
- Temperature monitoring system

## Emergency Equipment Locations

!!! danger "Emergency Information"
    Familiarize yourself with these locations before beginning work.

### Fire Safety
- **Fire Extinguishers:** Every 50 feet throughout facility
- **Fire Alarm Pulls:** Near all exits
- **Emergency Exits:** Clearly marked with illuminated signs

### First Aid
- **First Aid Kits:** 
    - Production area (near crush pad)
    - Cellar (main entrance)
    - Laboratory (wall mount)
    - Bottling line (supervisor office)

### Safety Equipment
- **Eyewash Stations:** Production, lab, and cellar
- **Safety Showers:** Production and lab areas
- **Spill Kits:** Chemical storage areas

### Emergency Contacts
- **Posted at:** Main office, production entrance, cellar entrance
- **Information includes:** Emergency services, management contacts, poison control

## Access and Security

### Working Hours
- **Standard Operations:** Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Harvest Season:** Extended hours, 7 days/week
- **Off-hours Access:** Requires management approval

### Key Card Access
All team members receive access cards programmed for their authorized areas. Report lost cards immediately to security.

### Visitor Policy
All visitors must:

- Sign in at the main office
- Wear visitor badge
- Be accompanied by authorized personnel
- Follow all safety protocols

## Parking and Amenities

### Parking
- **Employee Parking:** North lot
- **Visitor Parking:** Front lot
- **Loading Zone:** West side, warehouse entrance

### Break Rooms
- **Main break room:** Second floor, above production
- **Amenities:** Refrigerator, microwave, coffee maker, seating
- **Outdoor area:** Picnic tables on west patio

### Restrooms
- Production floor: East and west sides
- Office area: Second floor
- ADA-accessible facilities available
