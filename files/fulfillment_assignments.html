Contact Information

For questions or urgent requests specific to your order category, contact the assigned personnel directly:

  • Wine and Production Items: Contact Nick
  • Store Supplies: Contact Gina
  • Miscellaneous/Costco Items: Contact Tom

Order Routing

The order coordinator will automatically route order screenshots to the appropriate personnel based on item categories. Store locations do not need to contact fulfillment personnel directly unless there is an urgent or special request.
