Ordering Process Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions for the wine and supply ordering process.

Common Issues and Solutions

Problem: Cannot access the order form

Solution: Contact the order coordinator to verify your access permissions to the Google Sheet. Ensure you are logged into the correct Google account.

Problem: Missed the Tuesday 3:00 PM deadline

Solution: Your order will automatically roll to next week's cycle. For urgent items, contact the appropriate fulfillment personnel directly (Nick for wine/production, Gina for supplies, Tom for miscellaneous).

Problem: Incorrect items received

Solution: Review the "Order" column markings in your submitted form. Verify that the correct items were marked for fulfillment. Contact the fulfillment personnel to correct any discrepancies.

Problem: Need to change an order after submission

Solution: If the current time is before Tuesday 3:00 PM, you can edit the form directly. If it is after 3:00 PM Tuesday, contact the assigned fulfillment personnel directly to request changes.

Problem: Uncertain about par levels

Solution: Par levels are guidelines for minimum inventory. Use your best judgment based on anticipated demand, seasonal factors, and upcoming events. Contact Gina for clarification on supply par levels.

Problem: Don't know which category an item falls under

Solution: When in doubt, mark the item and add a note. The order coordinator will route it to the appropriate fulfillment personnel.

Escalation Process

If you encounter issues that cannot be resolved through the solutions above:

  1. Contact the order coordinator
  2. Document the issue and any attempted solutions
  3. For urgent matters, contact management directly
