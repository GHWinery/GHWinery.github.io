Ordering Supplies for the Winery

User Role: Store Leads or Managers
  1. Open the Winery Supply Order Form on Google Sheets.
  2. Use the form to take stock of inventory and identify any needed items.
  3. Complete the form by marking the items you need ordered.
    1. Enter the Current Stock of the item.
    2. Check the Order checkbox.
    3. For wine orders, enter the amount of Cases Ordered.
    4. Enter any additional details for the order.
  4. After completing your order for the week, send a message in the Square Production Chat confirming your order is finished.
    Note: The deadline to order for the week is 3 PM on Tuesday. Changes made to the spreadsheet after 3 PM might not be fulfilled until the following week.
  5. After orders are fulfilled and delivered, open the Winery Supply Order Form and mark items as received by unchecking the Order checkbox.
    If any items were not delivered, leave the Order checkbox marked.
