Fulfiling Wine Orders for Store Locations

User Role: Production Team
  1. Receive completed Order Form from Supply Chain Manager on Tuesday afternoon.
  2. Prepare delivery according to order form.
    1. Print order form.
    2. Gather wine and supplies for the store based on the order form and place near garage door for loading.
    3. After gathering all supplies, attach order form to the delivery.
    4. Repeat for every store's order.
  3. On Wednesday, load orders into van based on route.
  4. Deliver supplies to store.
  5. Give order form to store lead or manager for their records.
