Receiving Orders at Stores

Store Leads or Managers follow this procedure after an order is delivered to their store.

User Role: Store Leads or Managers
  1. After Production delivers order, use the printed Winery Supply Order Form to review the order.
  2. Note any missing items.
  3. Open the Winery Supply Order Form on Google Sheets and uncheck items that were delivered.
  4. If an item was not delivered, complete these actions.
    1. Leave the checkbox marked on the Winery Supply Order Form.
    2. Send an email to Steve at sales@greenhousewinery.com noting what items were not delivered.
      Steve will find out why items were not delivered and respond to your email with the reason.
